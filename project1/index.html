The End of the Line Tavern is located just outside the terminal of the final Seaport stop on the Metro East-West Train, only 100 yards to the beach. A perfect place to meet up with visiting friends and family, or maybe kill some time while you wait for your train! Whatever the case may be, we pride ourselves on our inviting atmosphere, friendly staff, great selection and a wide array of events and entertainment we're sure you'll love! So stop by next time you're in King's Point and see for yourself why we've been voted number one in town for 12 years running!

Our Story

We are a couple friends with a love for good food and good company. We had been imagining our tavern for years, and when the building at the Seaport Station came up for lease, we knew it was time. Both of us has missed more trains than we care to admit, and always the memory that sticks out is: "jeez, I sure wish there was somewhere I'd feel comfortable just hanging out for a few hours while I wait for the next one." So here we are. We strive to be a safe haven for anyone and everyone, a place to meet up with someone coming into town, a place to wait a few hours until the next train, or even just a favorite place in town to grab a drink or dinner with some pals. We wanted a universal sort of place that could appeal to all sorts of people and that's what the End of the Line is. We hope to see you soon!
